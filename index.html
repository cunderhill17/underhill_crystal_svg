Brand Icons

The Quattro icon set is designed as a visual expression of Italian refinement and contemporary craft. The icons balance natural edges with soft, flowing curves—a dialogue between structure and spontaneity that mirrors the aperitivo ritual itself: considered, yet effortlessly social. The forms feel tactile and organic, as if shaped by hand rather than machine, evoking ingredients, landscape, and tradition without becoming literal.

Rendered predominantly in a deep burgundy tone (HEX #800020), the icons draw from the chromatic world of rich botanicals, aged liqueurs, and evening light in Italy. This colour lends warmth, depth, and quiet confidence, reinforcing Quattro’s high-end positioning while remaining sensual and inviting. Together, the iconography creates a cohesive visual language that feels timeless yet modern—rooted in nature, elevated by design, and unmistakably Quattro.
